FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

If you are stuck somewhere, here are the detailed steps for each and every function and the feature on StreamGuru that will help you fix your problems and continue with the task on hand

1. What is the Streamguru Screen Recorder?

This is a free mobile application with the option to record your screen live with video, audio or both.

It also has various other functions such as editing and trimming of the videos and images. It can showcase your screen activities live on YouTube and also help you to explain the happenings on the screen with its writing tool to make a tutorial video.

It is also possible to make interactive videos that record yourself with the screen activities. You may make GIFs from your videos too.

These all can be further shared publically through various options.

2. How do I know about all the control buttons?

All the feature buttons are explained in the “Help” option of Settings.

  • Open the App
  • Select “Settings” on top right corner of the screen
  • Tap on “Other”
  • Choose “Help”

Further tap on the screen and know about all the feature buttons.

3. How can I control all the functions?

Functions can be controlled from the Notification Panel as well as the Control Buttons on your screen.

4. How to disable the control buttons while recording?

  • Open the App
  • Select “Settings” on top right corner of the screen
  • “On” the Hide Recording Controls

5. How will I control functions if my control buttons are disabled?

You can control all the functions from the notification panel itself by dragging it down.

6. How much to pay for using this app?

This app can be downloaded for free from Google Play store. It would use internet data when downloaded and streaming live on YouTube.

7. How can I do live YouTube Streaming?

Note: You need a YouTube Account

.
  • Select YouTube Live Button ( symbol ) from Control Buttons
  • Enter Stream Title after selecting the YouTube Account
  • Add Description for the video
  • Select your Stream Privacy for the video
  • Select Go Live

8. How to convert Videos to GIF?

  • Open the App
  • Tap on (Symbol)
  • Tap on Video to GIF
  • Select the video to convert
  • Tap on “Start”

9. How to record the screen?

  • From the Control Buttons select (Symbol)
  • Select “Start Now”
  • To directly start the recording the next time tick mark the “Don’t show again” box
  • Drag the Notification Panel or Tap on Control Button to Pause (Symbol) or Stop (Symbol) the recording.

10. How to stop or change the countdown timer?

  • Open the app
  • Tap on top right corner for Settings
  • Tap on “Control”
  • Tap on Countdown Timer and set as per your requirement

11. How to take a screenshot?

  • From the Control Buttons select (Symbol)
  • Select “Start Now”
  • To directly start the recording the next time tick mark the “Don’t show again” box
  • You can view the captured image in the Gallery (Symbol)

12. How to record only the audio?

  • From the Control Buttons select Others (Symbol)
  • From available 3 options select Audio (Symbol)
  • After the Count Down, the audio will be started to record
  • To Stop the Recording, select “Stop” from the Notification Panel or the Control Panel (if enabled)

13. How to change/check where everything is saved?

  • Open app
  • Select “Settings”
  • Under “Video” Section there is “Storage Location”

14. How to trim the video?

  • Go to the Gallery
  • Select (Symbol)
  • Tap on Trim Video
  • Select the video you want to trim
  • Select the Range
  • Tap on top right corner “Save” symbol

On the next screen on top right corner there will be further 3 options of: Trim, Share and Delete (symbols).

The trimmed video will be saved as per the path mentioned in Storage Location in Settings > Video inside the app.

15. How to rename the taken video, audio or screenshot image?

  • Open the app
  • Tap on the name of the item
  • Write another name
  • Tap on “Save”

16. How to trim the recorded videos?

  • Open the app
  • Go to videos gallery and locate the video you want to trim
  • Tap on “Trim (Scissors)” Symbol
  • Select the Range
  • Save the trimmed video from top right corner

17. How to Share or Delete the video/screenshot image/audio?

Share:
  • Open the app
  • Go to gallery and locate the video/screenshot image/audio you want to share
  • Tap on “Share (symbol)” and further select the mode of sharing like email, whatsapp, messenger, etc and further share it with anyone or all.
Delete
  • Open the app
  • Go to gallery and locate the video/screenshot image/audio you want to delete
  • Tap on “Delete (symbol)”
  • Select “Yes” to Delete on the pop up.

18. How to use and control the Explaining tool?

  • From the control Panel select “Symbol”
  • After the countdown you can start the tutorial video
  • You can “Stop” or “Pause” from the control buttons on the timer or the notification panel

25. How to set the orientation of the video?

  • Open App
  • Go to “Settings” (symbol) from top right corner
  • Select “Orientation”
  • Set as per requirement.

25. I do not want the audio in my recording, what to do?

  • Open App
  • Go to “Settings” (symbol) from top right corner
  • Disable “Record Audio” under “Video”
